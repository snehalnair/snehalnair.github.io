## Overview
This deck walks through a practical attribution modelling workflow, covering data collection, transformation, analytics, referral traffic segmentation, data quality, and attribution model selection.

## Summary
- **Data Collection & Session Stitching:** Guidance on identifying users across devices/sessions and handling cookie deletion.
- **Data Transformation & Business Goals:** Aligns transformations to the metrics needed for attribution.
- **Attribution Models:** Contrasts data-driven attribution (DDA) vs marketing mix models (MMM), including requirements and pitfalls.
- **Referral Traffic Segmentation:** Definitions, hurdles (messy URLs, spam, self-referrals), and a structured process to improve channel rules.
- **Data Quality & Audits:** Emphasizes planned audits to maintain reliable attribution over time.
