---
title: "Knowledge Governance (GraphRAG)"
excerpt: "Self-adaptive GraphRAG pipeline reducing hallucinations by 90% and tickets by 34%."
---

Situation: Knowledge answers were inconsistent across sources, increasing hallucinations and support tickets.

Task: Build a governance-focused RAG system that prioritizes trusted sources and propagates truth reliably.

Action:
- Implemented a five-stage GraphRAG pipeline with source authority tiers.
- Used Neo4j to manage entity and document relationships for conflict resolution.
- Added automated truth propagation and monitoring for drift.

Results:
- Hallucinations reduced by 90%.
- Support tickets reduced by 34%.
